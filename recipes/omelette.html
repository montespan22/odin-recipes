Omelette

Description

A classic egg technique and French recipe that can be filled with any garnish ingredients that you like.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • Any preferred garnish

Steps

  1. Breaks eggs in a bowl, add salt and pepper and whisk with a fork until smooth.
  2. Melt butter in a pan over medium-high heat.
  3. Add egg mixture to the pan and cook until creamy.
  4. Add preferred garnish and fold the omlette.
  5. Serve on a plate and add spices to taste.
